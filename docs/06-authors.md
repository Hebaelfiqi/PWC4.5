*Affiliations current as of July 2026; the linked profiles are authoritative.*

**Dr. Heba El-Fiqi** is a Senior Lecturer in Artificial Intelligence with the
School of Systems and Computing at UNSW Canberra, Australia. Her research
spans decentralised intelligent systems, swarm intelligence, representation
learning, and cognitive signal processing. She received her Ph.D. in Computer
Science from UNSW Canberra (2013) — the thesis this repository accompanies —
her M.Sc. in Computer Science from Cairo University (2008), and her B.Sc. in
Computer Science from Zagazig University (2003).
[UNSW profile](https://www.unsw.edu.au/staff/heba-el-fiqi) ·
[Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=6ySMBdQAAAAJ)

**Associate Professor Eleni Petraki** is an Associate Professor at the
University of Canberra, Australia, where she serves as Associate Dean for
Higher Degree Research. Her background is in applied linguistics, with
research contributions in conversation and discourse analysis, intercultural
communication, and language education.
[University of Canberra profile](https://researchprofiles.canberra.edu.au/en/persons/eleni-petraki/) ·
[Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com.au/citations?user=QAwizhcAAAAJ)

**Professor Hussein Abbass** is a Professor with the School of Systems and
Computing at UNSW Canberra, Australia. He was the Founding Editor-in-Chief of
the IEEE Transactions on Artificial Intelligence (2020–2024) and is a Fellow
of the IEEE, the Australian Computer Society, and the Operational Research
Society (UK). His research covers trusted autonomy, human–machine teaming,
and AI-enabled swarm systems.
